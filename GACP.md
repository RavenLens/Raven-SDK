# GACP (Graph Agent Communication Protocol)
It's a protocol allow to communicate between heerds/bevies of agents along their tasks and off tasks

## What can agents share among themselfs?
- knowledge
- skills
- what task are they processing
- stage in processing the task 

## What can agents get out of this communication?
- Do subtasks in background to speedup the process
- Share the ideas from reasoning process along the task is progressing -> onlly when the task idea is important
